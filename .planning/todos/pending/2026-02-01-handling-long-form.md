## Problem

User wants features optimized for long-form writing (novels, research papers, books).

## Solution

- Chapter/section management
- Manuscript view (continuous scroll of chapters)
- Chapter navigation panel
- Scene/beat tracking for fiction
- Research note linking
- Word count targets per chapter
- Compile/export entire manuscript
